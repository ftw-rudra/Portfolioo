01 The Story
WHO
I AM

I design experiences,
not just visuals.

Over the past two years, I've partnered with more than 100 clients as a freelance graphic designer, building impactful visual identities using Photoshop, Illustrator, and Figma. Every brief taught me something new about communication, emotion, and the quiet power of a well-placed weight.

But I've always been drawn to worlds you can step inside. That pull led me to teach myself game development and 3D modeling — using Unity and Blender to explore what happens when graphic design logic meets interactive space. I'm fascinated by the intersection: the moment a flat composition becomes something you navigate.

I'm a student and a freelancer simultaneously, which means I'm always learning by doing. Every project is a new language. Every brief is a new world to build.
